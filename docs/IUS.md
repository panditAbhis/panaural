# Intended Use Statement

Panaural is an acoustic drift-detection instrument. It listens to a mechanical system
through a contact or air microphone, learns the system's healthy spectral signature
during a documented calibration period, and reports drift from that baseline in plain language.

**Verdicts issued by Panaural are indicative, not diagnostic.**

Panaural does not replace certified inspection procedures (ISO 13373, ISO 20816).
All maintenance decisions shall be validated by qualified personnel.

## Classification

Class B — Non-safety-critical monitoring aid.

## Not for use in

- Life-safety systems
- Medical devices
- Nuclear systems
- Aviation
- As a replacement for certified inspection

## For use in

- Early-warning acoustic condition monitoring of industrial rotating machinery
- Decision-support alongside certified maintenance procedures
